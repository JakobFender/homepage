The Bachelor in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts provides
a strong foundation in machine learning, data science, and AI systems, combining theory with hands-on projects. The
program emphasizes applied problem-solving, preparing students to design, train, and deploy AI models in real-world
settings.
